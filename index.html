About Me

Hi, I'm Jesse Stoler. I'm from Pocatello, Idaho, and no, I don't need your pity. I started teaching myself to code about six months ago, and fell in love with it. It's both intellectually and creatively fulfilling.

I majored in journalism at Idaho State University. I still don't know why I was stupid enough to do that. Since then, most of my professional career has been working as a content marketer at various SEO/digital marketing agencies.

Beyond the professional, I love to write recreationally, hike, watch too much NBA basketball, and from time-to-time I will do stand-up (if I'm feeling good drunk enough).
